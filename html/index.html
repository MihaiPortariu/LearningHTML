World Content Accessibility Guidelines

WCAG 2.0
The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) are part of a series of web Accessibility Guidelines published by Accessibility Initiative (WAI) of the world wide web Consortium (W3C). The main International Standards Organization for the internet. They are a set of recommendations for making web content more accessible, primarily for people with disabilities, but also for all user’s agents, including highly limited devices, such as mobile phones.
